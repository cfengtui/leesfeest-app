3. Voice Processing (Deepgram)

‚ö†Ô∏è Note: If you enable automatic scoring mode, voice recordings are processed by Deepgram, a service based in the United States.

What happens to your voice data?

  • Voice recordings are sent to Deepgram in real-time for speech recognition
  • Deepgram converts audio to text and returns it
  • We do not permanently store audio recordings
  • Deepgram processes data according to their privacy policy

Your choice

You can choose manual scoring mode where no voice data is transmitted. This is the default setting. You can change your preference in the app settings.

4. Children and parental consent

Important for parents: Under Dutch GDPR implementation (UAVG), parental consent is required for processing personal data of children under 16 for online services.

By creating an account for your child, you confirm that you are the parent or legal guardian and consent to data processing as described in this privacy policy.

5. How long do we keep your data?

  • Account data: As long as your subscription is active
  • Reading results: As long as your account exists
  • Voice recordings: Not stored (real-time processing only)

After account termination, your data will be deleted within 30 days.

6. Your rights

Under GDPR, you have the following rights:

  • Right of access: Request what data we hold about you
  • Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to data portability: Receive your data in a standard format
  • Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw voice processing consent anytime via app settings

To exercise your rights, contact us using the email address above.

7. Security

We take appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal data:

  • Encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Secure password storage (hashing)
  • Limited access to data

8. Cookies and tracking

We do not use tracking cookies or advertisements. We only use functional storage (localStorage) to remember your login status and preferences.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. For significant changes, we will notify you via the app or email.

10. Complaints
